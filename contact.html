About

I'm Nata Machaladze, illustrator and Graphic designer based in Tbilisi, Georgia.

Having successfully completed my degree in communication design, I have gained invaluable experiences in the art of brand building. Currently, I am fully immersed in the world of modern, vibrant, and trendy designs, constantly striving to infuse them with my unique style.

Sometimes I work for myself and sometimes for clients such as Adobe, NY Times, Google Play, Spotify, YouTube Music, Xbox, Instagram, Facebook, wok stop, PSP and more.
